How to Play

Getting Started

  • Tap & Hold: Control your CrazyRubic as it spins.
  • Dodge Obstacles: Avoid lava blocks, meteor storms, and spinning flames.
  • Collect Boosts: Snag power-ups that grant bursts of speed or temporary invincibility.
  • Challenge Yourself: Survive rising difficulty levels, slay monsters for combos, and rack up massive scores!

Heroes & Planets

From celestial guardians to volcanic warriors, each Rubic hero boasts a unique theme and lore. Travel across different planets, each adding distinct visuals and lava types to test your skill.

Heroes

  • Tiger Stone Rubic – Fierce amber stripes conjure primal jungle spirits.
  • Silver Rubic – Lunar metal that reflects blows like moonlight.
  • Lapis Lazuli Rubic – Forged in ocean depths, harnesses tidal spells.
  • Ruby Rubic – Volcanic fury ignites embers, melting steel in seconds.
  • Malachite Rubic – Earth’s lifeforce regenerates wounds swiftly.
  • Unicorn Rubic – Radiant horn dissolves despair with starlight magic.
  • Amethyst Rubic – Born of astral shadows, bending reality with illusions.
  • Emerald Rubic – Commands forest vines and healing auras.
  • Gold Rubic – Solar flares embolden allies and scorch the wicked.
  • Mother Pearl Rubic – Moonlit foam illusions entrance foes in swirling pearls.
  • Sapphire Rubic – Glacial fortress forging frigid winds and snowstorms.
  • Turquoise Rubic – Desert illusions twist the battlefield into scorching mirages.
  • Diamond Rubic – Unbreakable cosmic gem that refracts every blow.
  • Pink Diamond Rubic – Healing glow so potent it transforms dark hearts.
  • Rainbow Rubic – Prismatic blasts warp reality with kaleidoscopic illusions.

Planets

Each planet you choose alters the environment’s color palette and lava type – from molten magma to toxic sludge or icy flows. Survive Mercury’s scorching surface, dodge acidic hazards on Venus, or leap through the frost-lava pits of Neptune!

  • Moon: Cratered satellite with lower gravity feel.
  • Mercury: Scorched and rocky, with standard magma.
  • Venus: Blazing greenhouse with toxic “magma_toxic” flows.
  • Mars: Rusty deserts, swirling dust storms, classic magma chaos.
  • Jupiter: Huge gas giant storms swirling in the background.
  • Saturn: Iconic rings and golden clouds, “magma_toxic” hazards.
  • Uranus: Aquamarine ice-lava fields, slick and treacherous.
  • Neptune: Deep azure with supersonic winds and “magma_ice”.
  • Sun: A blazing star of solar flares and molten ground.

Perks & Power-Ups

Enhance your runs with special perks and on-the-fly power-ups. Earn coins to unlock new perks, or watch ads for extra boosts.

  • Extra Life: Defy death once more! Automatically revives you upon losing a run. Great for extending record-breaking attempts.
  • Slowdown: Temporarily reduces all movement speed, making dodges easier when chaos peaks.
  • Shield: Grants a protective barrier that negates one hit from lava or obstacles.

Perks can be acquired with in-game gold or occasionally won by watching rewarded ads. Access the in-game Shop to purchase coin packs or subscribe to remove ads (and earn bonus heroes & planets).
